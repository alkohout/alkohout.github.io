Waves-in-ice

This project investigated how Southern Ocean waves break up sea ice, and what this means for ice extent in polar regions. Our research revealed several significant discoveries about wave-ice interactions.

IVF Genetic Mutation Study

We investigated the genetic mutations in IVF patients to determine the feasibility of developing a simple at-home test that could predict the likelihood of successful IVF treatments.

Board game logging

I developed a local webinterface connected to a SQL database to log and view statistics of played boardgames.
